Our Story

Mupy started in early 2019 as a python coding club at Miami University of Ohio. Our goal was simple. We realized there were hardly any resources on campus to help students in majors other than computer science to learn how to code. We designed and redisigned a python course for beginers until it properly fit the needs of our students.

Since then, we've worked to expand beyond python, and now offer specialized courses based on the needs and experience of each student. With our unique tools and hands on teaching expeince, there's never been a better place to learn to code.
